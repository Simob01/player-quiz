# Privacy Policy for Football IQ: Guess the Player

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Huge Dreamer ("we", "our", or "us") provides the mobile game **Football IQ: Guess the Player** (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

## Summary

The App is a football trivia and player-guessing game. It does not require an account, login, subscription, payment, name, email address, or phone number. Game progress is stored locally on your device. The App uses Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads, and Google may collect device and advertising identifiers to provide, measure, and improve ads.

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly

We do not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or account credentials.

### Game progress stored on your device

The App stores game progress locally using on-device storage such as SharedPreferences. This may include:

- XP, level, rank, reputation, and Scout Credits
- Games played, wins, streaks, correct guesses, and accuracy
- Intel/hint usage
- Collected players and album progress
- Daily mission, weekly challenge, and login reward progress
- Basic app settings such as sound, haptic, and gameplay preferences

This local data is stored on your device and is not sent to Huge Dreamer servers.

### Advertising and device information

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process information such as:

- Device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID where available
- App set ID and other device or app identifiers
- IP address, approximate location inferred from IP address, device type, operating system, and app information
- Ad interactions, impressions, clicks, diagnostics, and performance data

Google uses this information to provide ads, limit fraud and abuse, measure ad performance, and support personalized or non-personalized advertising depending on your settings, region, and consent choices.

You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID and manage ad personalization in your device settings.

## How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

- To operate the game and save your progress locally
- To show gameplay rewards, collection progress, ranks, and missions
- To display and manage advertising through Google AdMob
- To support rewarded ad flows, such as earning Scout Credits or unlocking optional Intel
- To improve app stability, performance, and user experience
- To comply with legal, security, and Google Play policy requirements

## Advertising Through Google AdMob

The App includes:

- Banner ads on Home, Album, and Career screens
- Interstitial ads after configured completed Scout Run intervals
- Rewarded ads for optional rewards such as Scout Credits, Intel when out of credits, reward doubling, or a failed-run continue

The App does not show banner ads during active Scout Run gameplay.

Rewarded ad rewards are granted only after the official rewarded-ad completion callback is received from the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

Google may use the Android Advertising ID and other data for ad delivery and measurement. For more information, review:

- Google Privacy Policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
- Google AdMob: <https://admob.google.com/>
- Google Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure: <https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy/play-data-disclosure>

## Third-Party Services

The App uses or is prepared to use the following third-party services:

### Google AdMob

Used to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements. Google may collect advertising identifiers, device information, diagnostics, and ad interaction data as described above.

### Firebase Analytics

The App contains future-ready analytics hooks, but no active Firebase Analytics backend is configured in the current release. If Firebase Analytics is enabled in a future release, this Privacy Policy and Google Play disclosures will be updated before collecting analytics data through Firebase.

## Data Sharing

Huge Dreamer does not sell your personal information.

We do not share your local game progress with third parties. Advertising-related data may be collected and processed by Google through the Google Mobile Ads SDK according to Google's policies and your device settings.

We may disclose information if required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable government requests.

## Data Retention

Local game progress remains on your device until you delete the App, clear the App's data, or reset your device. Huge Dreamer does not maintain a server copy of your local progress.

Advertising data collected by Google is retained according to Google's own retention policies.

## Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of certain information.

Because the App does not require an account and does not store your game progress on Huge Dreamer servers, you can delete local game data by:

- Clearing the App's storage/data in Android settings
- Uninstalling the App

You can manage advertising preferences by:

- Resetting or deleting your Android Advertising ID
- Turning off ad personalization in Android or Google settings
- Using any consent or privacy controls shown by Google or your device

You may contact us at **cahsforphones48@gmail.com** with privacy questions or requests.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and is not intended for users who are under the minimum age required to use digital services in their country. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, contact us at **cahsforphones48@gmail.com** and we will take appropriate steps.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information handled by the App. Local game progress is stored on your device. Advertising data transmitted by Google Mobile Ads SDK is handled by Google and is encrypted in transit according to Google's documentation.

## International Processing

Google and its service providers may process advertising-related information on servers located in different countries. Your information may be processed outside your country of residence according to Google's policies and applicable law.
