Privacy Policy

Football IQ: Guess the Player

Effective July 20, 2026 No account required AdMob supported

Summary

Huge Dreamer ("we", "our", or "us") provides the mobile game Football IQ: Guess the Player (the "App"). The App does not require an account, login, subscription, payment, name, email address, or phone number. Game progress is stored locally on your device. The App uses Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads, and Google may collect device and advertising identifiers to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly. We do not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or account credentials.

Game progress stored on your device. The App stores game progress locally using on-device storage such as SharedPreferences. This may include XP, level, rank, reputation, Scout Credits, games played, wins, streaks, correct guesses, accuracy, Intel/hint usage, collected players, album progress, missions, login rewards, and basic app preferences.

This local data is stored on your device and is not sent to Huge Dreamer servers.

Advertising and device information. The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process device identifiers, Android Advertising ID where available, app set ID, IP address, approximate location inferred from IP address, device type, operating system, app information, ad interactions, impressions, clicks, diagnostics, and performance data.

How We Use Information

  • To operate the game and save your progress locally.
  • To show gameplay rewards, collection progress, ranks, and missions.
  • To display and manage advertising through Google AdMob.
  • To support rewarded ad flows, such as earning Scout Credits or unlocking optional Intel.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal, security, and Google Play policy requirements.

Advertising Through Google AdMob

The App includes banner ads on Home, Album, and Career screens; interstitial ads after configured completed Scout Run intervals; and rewarded ads for optional rewards such as Scout Credits, Intel when out of credits, reward doubling, or a failed-run continue.

The App does not show banner ads during active Scout Run gameplay. Rewarded ad rewards are granted only after the official rewarded-ad completion callback is received from the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

Google may use the Android Advertising ID and other data for ad delivery and measurement. You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID and manage ad personalization in your device settings.

Data Sharing

Huge Dreamer does not sell your personal information.

We do not share your local game progress with third parties. Advertising-related data may be collected and processed by Google through the Google Mobile Ads SDK according to Google's policies and your device settings.

We may disclose information if required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable government requests.

Data Retention

Local game progress remains on your device until you delete the App, clear the App's data, or reset your device. Huge Dreamer does not maintain a server copy of your local progress.

Advertising data collected by Google is retained according to Google's own retention policies.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of certain information.

Because the App does not require an account and does not store your game progress on Huge Dreamer servers, you can delete local game data by clearing the App's storage/data in Android settings or uninstalling the App.

You can manage advertising preferences by resetting or deleting your Android Advertising ID, turning off ad personalization in Android or Google settings, and using any consent or privacy controls shown by Google or your device.
